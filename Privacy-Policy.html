Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Welcome to TGNext. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Google account information (if using Google Sign-In)

1.2 Usage Data

  • Telegram bot tokens and group IDs you add to the service
  • AI API keys you provide (stored securely and encrypted)
  • Token balance and transaction history

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and crash reports
  • General usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the TGNext service
  • Process your purchases and manage your subscription
  • Connect your Telegram bots with AI services
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important service notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All sensitive data (API keys, bot tokens) is encrypted at rest
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
  • Your data is stored on protected servers with limited access
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

TGNext integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Telegram API: To manage your bots and groups
  • OpenAI / Google Gemini / DeepSeek: To process AI requests
  • RevenueCat: To manage subscriptions and purchases
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (optional)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

TGNext is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.
